# Keepa Countable
[Live App](https://walizamanee.github.io/KeepaCountable/)

> *Master your time, conquer your tasks.*

Keepa Countable is a powerful, offline-first productivity dashboard designed to gamify your work and help you build consistent habits. It combines a Todo list, Pomodoro Timer, Habit Tracker, and detailed Time Logging into a beautiful, customizable interface.

## Key Features

### Task Management
-   **Task List:** Add tasks with deadlines and tags.
-   **Smart Sorting:** Tasks are automatically sorted by urgency.
-   **Completed Archive:** Searchable history of all your conquered missions.

### Focus Timer (Pomodoro)
-   **Drift-Proof:** Accurate timer that works even if you switch tabs.
-   **Persistence:** Refreshed the page? Your timer resumes exactly where you left off.
-   **Visuals:** Beautiful progress ring and motivational quotes that cycle every minute.

### Habit Tracker
-   **Heatmap:** Visualize your consistency with GitHub-style contribution graphs.
-   **Daily Logging:** Click to log intensity levels (1-4) for each habit.

### Time Logging & Analytics
-   **Daily Breakdown:** Log activities (Coding, Reading, etc.) and see a pie chart of your day.
-   **History:** View past days' logs and stats with the "View Past Days" feature.

### Gamification System
**Level Up Your Productivity!**
-   **Ranks:** Start at **Rank 0**. Maintain a streak to level up!
    -   **Rank 1:** Requires 4 Day Streak.
    -   **Rank 15:** The ultimate goal (60+ Day Streak).
-   **Streaks:** Log **5+ hours** of activity daily to keep your streak alive.
-   **Rewards:** Earn XP multipliers and badges as you progress.
-   **Protection:** Buy "Streak Freezes" with XP to protect your progress on busy days.

## How to Run

Keepa Countable is a pure web application. **No installation or server required!**

1.  Download the project folder.
2.  Locate `index.html`.
3.  **Double-click `index.html`** to open it in your favorite web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
4.  That's it! Your data is saved automatically to your browser's Local Storage.

## Customization Guide

Click the **Settings Icon** in the top-right to personalize your experience.

### Custom Wallpapers
Make the dashboard yours by adding custom backgrounds.
1.  Go to **Settings > Wallpaper**.
2.  In the **"Custom Image (Path)"** box, enter:
    -   A clearer path to a local image (e.g., `wallpapers/my-image.jpg` if you put it in the project folder).
    -   Or a web URL (e.g., `https://example.com/image.jpg`).
3.  Click **Apply**.

### Custom Quotes
Add your own motivation to the rotation.
1.  Go to **Settings > Custom Quotes**.
2.  Type your quote and click **Add**.
3.  It will now appear randomly in the header and timer!

### Themes
Choose from 8 beautiful color themes (4 Light, 4 Dark):
-   **Nature** (Green/Fresh)
-   **Ocean** (Blue/Calm)
-   **Sunset** (Pink/Warm)
-   **Lavender** (Purple/Creative)
-   **Night** (Dark Blue)
-   **Forest** (Dark Green)
-   **Crimson** (Dark Red)
-   **Midnight** (Dark Purple)

## Data Backup & Privacy

**Your data stays on your device.** We do not send anything to the cloud.

-   **Export Data:** Go to Settings and click **Export Data** to download a `.json` backup of your tasks, stats, and history.
-   **Import Data:** Restore a backup file to pick up where you left off (supports strict validation to prevent errors).

## "Extreme Hardening"
This app has been engineered for stability:
-   **Audio:** Uses a smart audio engine that won't crash your browser.
-   **Validation:** Prevents crashes from corrupted data or massive text inputs.
-   **Safety:** Your focus timer state is protected against accidental reloads.
